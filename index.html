About Krazi Magents

Krazi Magnets was established in 1994 from humble beginnings as a home-based business specializing in presentation graphics and print broking we stumbled across raw magnetic material, which led to the challenge of producing decorative fridge magnets for advertising. 

Today Krazi Magnets prides itself in the manufacture of high-quality magnets and on-time delivery, to anyone who should require magnets and is always striving to improve the product and come up with more ideas to satisfy the advertising market.
